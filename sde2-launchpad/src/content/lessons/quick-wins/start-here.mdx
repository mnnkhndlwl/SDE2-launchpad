These are the precise things that came up in your recent interviews. They're not deep weaknesses — they're quick fixes. Clear them in the first few days and start the 8-week plan from a position of confidence.

## Your checklist

1. **Two-pointer "move zeros"** — the array-compaction pattern you stumbled on. → [Move Zeroes lesson](/lessons/quick-wins/move-zeros)
2. **The JS prototype chain** — `__proto__` vs `prototype`, and why `class` is sugar. → [Prototype chain](/lessons/javascript/prototype-chain)
3. **`useImperativeHandle` + `forwardRef`** — exposing methods from a child via ref. → [useImperativeHandle](/lessons/react-native/imperative-handle)
4. **RN architecture** — bridge vs JSI/Fabric, the three threads. → [RN architecture](/lessons/react-native/architecture)
5. **Frontend system design** — a repeatable framework so the format isn't new. → [Frontend framework](/lessons/system-design/frontend-framework)

## How to use this platform

- **Read** a lesson, then play with its **visualizer** — step through, change the input, watch it run.
- Hit **＋ Add to flashcards** on anything you want to retain; the [flashcards](/practice/flashcards) page resurfaces it on a spaced schedule.
- Mark **mastery** (Learned → Practiced → Can teach) to track real understanding, not just "read it."
- Drill out loud in [Mock Mode](/practice/mock), and log every miss in your [Mistakes Log](/mistakes).
- Everything saves locally and works offline — install it as an app from your browser's address bar.
