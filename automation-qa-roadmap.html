Automation QA → SDET Roadmap

A complete, no-fluff path from manual tester to full-fledged automation engineer / SDET — built for the 2026 market. A 12-week plan plus deep topic tracks covering one language, Selenium & Playwright, API testing, framework design, CI/CD, performance, AI-assisted testing, mobile, plus cheat sheets, certifications and interview prep. Check things off — progress saves automatically in this browser.

Who this is for: manual testers moving to automation, fresh QA aspirants, or developers pivoting into test engineering. The 2026 reality: Playwright adoption (~45%) has overtaken Selenium (~22%) and pays 5–15% more; engineers who know both earn 15–25% more. API/integration tests are now more in-demand than UI tests. CI/CD appears in ~89% of QA roles, and AI-assisted testing (self-healing, LLM test generation) is the fastest-rising skill. SDET = automation + APIs + CI/CD + cloud + AI fluency, on top of solid test fundamentals. Rule of the road: pick one language (Java, Python, or JS/TS) and go deep — don't dabble in three.
